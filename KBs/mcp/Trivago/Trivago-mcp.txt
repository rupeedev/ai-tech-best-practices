  Trivago MCP allows you to:
  - Search hotels and accommodations
  - Compare prices across providers
  - Get hotel details and reviews
  - Find deals and offers
  - Access location-based searches

## Common Commands

### Search Hotels
mcp__trivago__search_hotels
  - destination: "New York"
  - check_in: "2025-01-15"
  - check_out: "2025-01-18"
  - guests: 2

### Get Hotel Details
mcp__trivago__get_hotel
  - hotel_id: "12345"

### Search by Location
mcp__trivago__search_by_location
  - latitude: 40.7128
  - longitude: -74.0060
  - radius: 5

### Compare Prices
mcp__trivago__compare_prices
  - hotel_id: "12345"
  - check_in: "2025-01-15"
  - check_out: "2025-01-18"

### Get Reviews
mcp__trivago__get_reviews
  - hotel_id: "12345"
  - limit: 20

### Search Deals
mcp__trivago__search_deals
  - destination: "Paris"
  - max_price: 200
  - rating_min: 4
